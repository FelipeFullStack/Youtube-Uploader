## âš™ï¸ How to Use Youtube-Uploader
Once you have installed Youtube-Uploader, follow these steps to set up and start uploading your videos:

1. **Setup Watched Folder:**
   Open the app and navigate to the settings. Here, you can specify a folder on your computer. This folder will be watched for new video files. Any videos you place here will be automatically uploaded to your YouTube channel.

4. **Watch for New Videos:**
   Once everything is set, the app will watch the specified folder for any new videos. When a new video appears, it will automatically upload it to your YouTube channel.
